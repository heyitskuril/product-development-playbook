# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

This is an open, welcoming space for developers, product builders, and learners at every level.

We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful and constructive experience for everyone, regardless of age, background, experience level, nationality, or identity.

---

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Give and receive feedback graciously
- Focus on what is best for the community and the quality of the guide
- Assume good intent from other contributors

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Dismissive, insulting, or demeaning comments
- Personal attacks or harassment of any kind
- Spam, self-promotion, or off-topic content
- Deliberately submitting false or misleading information

---

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainer directly via GitHub.

The project maintainer reserves the right to remove comments, close issues, or block contributors who violate this code of conduct.
